# Plan CEO Review

Strategic/founder-mode plan review.

## When to Use

- Strategic planning sessions
- Plan review and evaluation
- User questions scope or ambition
- Expanding or rethinking a plan

## Modes

1. **SCOPE EXPANSION** - Dream big
2. **SELECTIVE EXPANSION** - Hold scope + cherry-pick expansions
3. **HOLD SCOPE** - Maximum rigor
4. **SCOPE REDUCTION** - Strip to essentials

## Focus

- Find the 10-star product
- Challenge premises
- Expand scope when it creates a better product
